4. Luxury Terminal Infrastructure

BTG Terminal - The Gold Standard

The BTG Terminal represents the pinnacle of luxury terminal infrastructure, combining aesthetic beauty with comprehensive amenities.

What Makes BTG Terminal So Great?

Tangible Customer Experience

  • Aesthetically Beautiful: Modern, elegant, warm, understated
  • Luxury Amenities: Showers, Revo/DP Lounge, meeting rooms, culinary & cocktail experiences
  • Private: Private entrance, private suite, private transfer to/from aircraft

Intangible Customer Experience

  • Stress-Free: Assisted check-in & bag drop-off, dedicated concierge
