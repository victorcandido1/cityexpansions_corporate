üßÆ Corporate Score Formula

We developed a Weighted Geometric Mean score using four normalized components:

Corporate Score = Revenue0.40 √ó Employment0.20 √ó Payroll/Emp0.20 √ó Distance¬≤0.20
Component Weight Description Data Source
Revenue 40% Estimated revenue using NAICS-specific coefficients Census Economic Census
Employment 20% Total number of employees Census CBP 2021
Payroll/Employee 20% Average salary - productivity proxy REAL DATA Census CBP 2021
Distance to Airport¬≤ 20% Proximity to major hub (closer = higher score) Geodesic calculation

Why Payroll/Employee? This is 100% real data from Census (not estimated), and higher salaries correlate with higher-skilled, more productive workers. San Francisco leads with $90k avg, while Dallas has $47k.
