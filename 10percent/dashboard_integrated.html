üìê Score Calculation Methodology

Both scores use Weighted Geometric Mean with global normalization (0-1) across all US ZIP codes.

üè† HOUSEHOLD GEOMETRIC SCORE
Score = (IRS_Wealth^0.50 √ó Time¬≤^0.20 √ó Pop200k^0.20 √ó Density^0.10)

Components:
‚Ä¢ IRS Wealth Proxy (50%): AGI, Capital Gains, Dividends, Business Income per return

‚Ä¢ Population $200k+ (20%): Number of high-income households
‚Ä¢ Density (10%): High-income households per km¬≤

üíº CORPORATE GEOMETRIC SCORE
Score = (Revenue^0.40 √ó Employment^0.20 √ó Payroll/Emp^0.20 √ó Distance¬≤^0.20)

Components:
‚Ä¢ Revenue (40%): Estimated total revenue (normalized)
‚Ä¢ Employment (20%): Total number of employees (normalized)
‚Ä¢ Payroll per Employee (20%): Census CBP real data - productivity proxy (normalized)
‚Ä¢ Distance to Airport¬≤ (20%): Geodesic distance squared (normalized)
